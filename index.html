About

Using OCR (Optical Character Recognition), you can extracts text from images. Just upload your photos, click submit, and instantly receive your text file. Powered by tesseract-ocr, a Google-backed open-source software, our tool supports multiple languages, enabling text extraction in various languages. Once you're done, you can download the text as a file or copy it to the clipboard.
